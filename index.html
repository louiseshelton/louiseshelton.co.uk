About

I am currently a third year student of Filmmaking and Creative Media at UWE_Film,
ready to embark on my next adventure in the media industry.

From an early age I was captivated by high quality film and television productions and in particular period dramas, such as ‘Pride and Prejudice’. My passion for outdoor activities, including white water kayaking, mountaineering and water sports, has led to my interest in wildlife and environmental documentaries and how, through different medias, these can impact the way the general public think and behave.

During my time at College and then at University, I have gained experience in many aspects of film production and I am looking forward to further developing skills in producing, directing and production design in the future. Ultimately, I hope to influence people’s attitudes and the responses of industry and governments to environmental threats.

A Drop In The Ocean

Previous Next

An interactive documentary

My aim for this interactive project ‘A Drop In The Ocean’ is to raise awareness about the problem of plastic waste and encourage people to make a difference before it's too late.
I hope to motivate and inspire audiences to want to adapt their lifestyles slightly, to make a larger impact on the quantity of plastic wasted and inevitably ending up in our oceans.
I am the Director and Co-Producer of this project, which consists of three short documentaries embedded in an interactive interface, that enables the audience to explore the online story in which ever way they prefer.

Love is not Blind

A romantic drama about arranged marriage.

The bride to be is forced into an arranged marriage by her parents, even though she is in love with another man. The story is about the struggles of the couple in their newly married life.
I was a Production Designer for this short film. Creating the wedding scene for the film set was a challenging but rewarding experience and I enjoyed every moment of it.

A Tough Cull

Previous Next

An interactive documentary about the problems surrounding the Badger Cull.


‘A Tough Cull’ is an interactive documentary, that shows a range of perspectives on how to control the TB problems in cattle in the UK. For this project I was the Producer. ​
Alongside the main documentary, the interactive sections allow the audience to observe the people in the documentary giving their points of view about the issues surrounding the badger cull, which helps the audience to come to their own conclusions about the cull.

The Legend of Lucy Drake

Previous Next

An immersive and interactive horror experience.


Members of the public were invited to follow the story of a girl called Gemma. Each day the audience would be sent clues to help them to solve the mystery of 'the Legend of Lucy Drake'; this was followed by a live event, where the participants were actively involved in the conclusion of the story.
For this project I was a Production Designer, which involved setting the stage for the live event, in addition to preparing and decapitating dolls for the purpose of publicity. I helped to hide pieces of these dolls around Bristol with a web address attached, in order to attract our audience to this experience.

Radio Silence

A horror radio podcast


I was the producer for Radio Silence, which is a horror Podcast based in a Radio broadcasting studio. Maggie, a news reporter, is out on location interviewing members of the public about a summer fete, when suddenly things take a turn for the worst. The distant screams that Maggie can hear are getting closer and havoc is wreaked on what was a fun day out. ​
