A Drop In The Ocean

An interactive documentary

Aiming to motivate and inspire audiences to want to adapt their lifestyles slightly to make a larger impact on the quantity of plastic wasted and inevitably ending up in our Oceans. This is a project I have been directing and co-producing.
The project consists of three short documentaries embedded in an interactive interface, the audience can explore the online story in which ever way they prefer.
My aim for this project is awareness about the plastic waste and encourage people to make a difference before it's too late.

Love is not Blind

A romantic drama about arranged marriage.

The bride to be is forced into an arranged marriage by her parents even though she is in love with another. The story is about the struggles of the couple in their newly married life.
I was one of the production designers for this short film, creating the wedding scene was a challenging but rewarding experience and enjoyed every moment of it.

The Legend of Lucy Drake

An immersive and interactive horror experience.


Members of the public were invited to follow the story of a girl called Gemma. Each day the audience would be sent clues so they could help solve the mystery of the Legend of Lucy Drake, this ended with a live event where the participants could get actively involved in concluding the story.
For this project I was a production designer, this involved setting the stage for the live event and decapitating dolls. Pieces of the dolls were hidden around Bristol with a web address attached as a way of attracting an audience.
